The Song of Achilles

By Madeline Miller

The writing was lyrical and poetic without feeling forced or unnatural. Complex emotions were described perfectly.

Although the middle 40% went by slow, in hindsight it feels necessary and appropriate. It allowed enogh time to show the progress of change between our main characters.

Ask for Andrea

By Noelle W. Ihli

It was a bit predictable, and I expected more from the ending. Some parts I really enjoyed, but it is definitely forgettable.
